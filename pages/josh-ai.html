Josh.ai

Josh.ai provides complex whole home control for high-end homes. They’re best known for their voice control, but they offer a lot more than that. Spending four years there on a design team of two, I touched pretty much everything in the company — web & mobile apps, marketing, swag, print design, and even some 3D hardware design renders. I led the design of the web portal, which is used by home technology professionals to set up and manage their clients’ homes. It’s like the “power user” Josh product.

Industrial Design

I led product and industrial design for both Josh Nano and Josh Core. That included pencil & paper exploration, color, material, finishing, product packaging, and 3D product render videos. I also led a top-secret design project that can’t be shared without an NDA. If you’re interested, let me know.

Brand & Marketing

I worked on a lot of branding, swag and other marketing for Josh.ai. With the yearly CEDIA expo came lots of opportunities for advertising, booth creation, t-shirts, stickers, brochures and more.
